Key Features:

    Development Roadmap

    The SBKE will be developed in four distinct phases over 24 months. Use the filters to explore the timeline and hover over tasks for more details.

    Transformative Impact

    By unifying data and leveraging AI, the SBKE will revolutionize space biology research and mission planning.

    🚀

    Accelerated Discovery

    Enable literature-based discovery and knowledge synthesis, connecting disparate findings to reveal new scientific insights and research directions.

    💡

    AI-Powered Hypotheses

    Leverage AI to detect complex patterns in multi-omics and physiological data, generating novel, testable hypotheses for future experiments.

    🛰️

    Smarter Mission Planning

    Provide data-driven tools for mission risk assessment and resource optimization, enhancing crew safety and success for long-duration spaceflight.
